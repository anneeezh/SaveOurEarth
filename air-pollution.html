Air Pollution

As society develops and humans exploit the environment in which
they live in, the quality of the most important resource on which
our survival depends, the air, is being degraded.



Have you ever wondered where the wisps of black smoke from the chimneys of the factories you pass along the highway will go?



Or have you ever wondered where the exhaust from the back of a car goes?

The answer is in the air you breathe.

As industrialization developed, people are becoming aware that the air they breathe is being polluted.

About 4.2 million people died everyyear as a result of ambient air pollution, while more than 90% of people worldwide reside in areas with polluted air.
